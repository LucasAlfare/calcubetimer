What follows is a list of all DyamicStrings accessible within CCT. To distinguish them from plaintext, surround them with $$ and $$. To print $$pops$$ as plaintext, just use \$\$pops\$\$ Some keys may require a numeric argument specifying which RA you are interested in. Remember that CCT keeps track of all the statistics for two rolling average lengths. For example, to get the latest average for a given RA number, use $$currentAverage0$$ or $$currentAverage1$$.
